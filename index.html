Who is a Kinesiologist?

Who is a Kinesiologist?

Kinesiology is a study of body movement. A kinesiologist applies science of biomechanics, anatomy, physiology and neuroscience to provide services such as active rehabilitation. A kinesiologist creates customized exercise rehabilitation programs based on the requirements and injuries in order to recover from soft tissue injuries.

What kind of injuries are treated?

Injuries treated, but not limited to:

  • Musculoskeletal issues
  • Soft tissue damages
  • Joint problems
  • Low back pain
  • Psychological

What are the benefits of Active Rehab?

Benefits of Active Rehab

Active Rehab promotes an increase in the range of motion and strength. It helps maintain, rehabilitate and enhance movement and performance. Not only this, physical activity promotes brain function that enhances cognitive activity and helps with psychological barriers faced by a number of people recovering during the accident like depression and anxiety etc.

How to get started?

Get Started

Your doctor, physiotherapist or chiropractor can provide the recommendation. If you recently sustained MVA, you are pre-approved for 12 Kin sessions including initial report for the first 12 weeks after your crash. Visit the following website for more information:

What to expect during your first appointment?

On your first appointment

The first appointment is an initial assessment. Kinesiologist will perform a series of range of motion and strength assessment activities. Questions regarding injuries or other related questions regarding the body and injuries will also be asked to better under the body’s current situation. It is advised to wear comfortable clothes and closed toe shoes/athletic shoes so you can perform the activities as needed.
Exercises will start in the follow-up sessions.

What is Community Kinesiologist?

Community Kinesiologist

Kinesiologist on road helps provide services at the many of the public recreation centers usually located close to the client. Home visits can also be arranged depending on your unique requirements.
